ABOUT

Several million tonnes of small hair are generated from hair salons across our country with an average population of 135 crores. We at Blak Diamond focus on using this waste of a resource into something useful. As an initiative of BookMyHairstylist (hyperlink), a salon booking application, we tend to add additional income to the salon owners. So basically we collect hair from the salons, process it, convert it into fertilizers and give it away at cheapest possible prices. Our Biochemists have worked hard to create a fertilizer blend that is completely organic and does not use chemicals in any part of the process.




WHY ORGANIC FERTILIZERS ?


Soil is the medium for the growth of crops and its fertility is more important for the yield. Our farmers have been using chemical fertilizers for a long time due to its high yield. But the continuous use of chemical fertilizers changes the nature of the soil. It changes the soil pH to acidity. The presence of more acidity in the soil creates a damaging effect known as soil friability. It ultimately leads to lower yield.

Chemical fertilizers are like medication, we take them only when we are sick and we canâ€™t consume just medicines as our food. Organic fertilizers are the nutrient-rich food which will increase the fertility of the soil and the yield. We are preparing it from natural sources that can be easily utilized by the plants. The nutrients in our fertilizer will maximize the growth and yield of the plant. Our fertilizer is rich in organic nitrogen, which is readily available for the plants. Our fertilizer will also increase the microbial population in the soil, which will further help to fix the atmospheric nitrogen in the soil. The fixed nitrogen can be utilized by the plants for their growth. Nowadays organic farming is getting huge interest in the market. Many dealers are available to buy the vegetables and crops grown using organic fertilizer.
